Project Notes

Why this exists

Prototype by Jack. Built as an interaction design study for approachable Korean learning.

Question

Can beginner Korean feel calmer when the first session behaves like a guided conversation rather than a dashboard of practice modes?

Hypothesis

If the app starts with one phrase, one check, and one scenario, a newcomer can feel oriented before seeing deeper tools.

Prototype

The experiment combines scenario missions, a voice companion, phrase details, adaptive review, and a short demo walkthrough.

Expected outcome

The design aims to create a directed, low-pressure first impression. This remains a prototype hypothesis until tested with new learners.
