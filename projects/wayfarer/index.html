EDITOR'S PICKS

Three guides worth getting lost in

Hand-picked this season — each one a small world to wander before you go.

Shizuka
What should I do on my first morning?
Wake before six and walk to the temple while the streets are still grey-blue. The bell carries across the valley; you'll have the moss gardens entirely to yourself.
Where do locals actually eat?
Skip the guidebook lists. The tea house above the valley has framed the same view since 1763 — order matcha and a seasonal wagashi, and stay until the light shifts.
How long should I stay?
A season, not a week. Stay long enough that the rice fields change colour while you watch and the baker sets aside your loaf without asking.
