Why contribute

Wayfarer only works if the guides are real. Here's the deal for the people who write them.

01

Your voice, kept whole

Guides aren't flattened into a template. Your design, your tone, your point of view — the way the five founding guides each feel like their own place.

02

Readers, not buyers

People come to Wayfarer to get lost reading. Your guide is read and followed, not sold. Community guides stay free and independent.

03

Founding contributors first

The earliest writers shape what Wayfarer becomes. Join the waitlist now and you're in the first room when publishing opens.
