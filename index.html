I am a Toronto-based designer and illustrator working across editorial design, campaign concepts, illustration, and visual storytelling. My Creative Industries background at Toronto Metropolitan University gives me a mix of design craft, business context, and cultural strategy.

I am open to junior designer, visual designer, and editorial design roles in studio or in-house teams, especially where concept, image-making, and clear design rationale matter.
