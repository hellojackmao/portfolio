About me

Jack
Mao

I am a Toronto-based designer and illustrator working across editorial design, campaign concepts, illustration, and visual storytelling — and when a project calls for it, I build the interactive side myself, designing and shipping it as working software. My Creative Industries background at Toronto Metropolitan University gives me a mix of design craft, business context, and cultural strategy.

I am open to designer, visual designer, and product design roles in studio or in-house teams — especially where concept, image-making, and the ability to take an idea all the way to a working interface all matter.
